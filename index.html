Autobiography

My name is Luthelle L. Fernandez, and I was born on October 14, 2004, in Muntinlupa City, where I was also raised. Growing up in a household where both of my parents are high school Math teachers, I was exposed to use computers that nurtured curiosity in graphic designing starting with creating PowerPoints and I enjoyed exploring the animations, transitions, and tools it provided, which became my first hands-on experience with digital creativity.

As I grew older, my passion and curiosity for graphics designing deepened. Entering a Science High School, sparked my interest in the world of web development. I was captivated by the process of how coding bring ideas to life.
